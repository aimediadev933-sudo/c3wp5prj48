About APOCNET

Oral cancer remains a significant health burden across many Asia-Pacific countries, where incidence rates are among the highest globally. APOCNET aims to establish a regional collaborative network across six countries to address the barriers to early detection.

Our project focuses on leveraging the TEIN*CC network infrastructure to support the creation of scalable, sustainable training and detection platforms, ensuring equitable access to early diagnosis tools and collaborative research, especially in low- and middle-income countries.

Our Mission

To build a collaborative regional network (APOCNET) for addressing barriers to early detection of oral cancer in Asia-Pacific using shared resources, digital technologies, and capacity-building programs.
